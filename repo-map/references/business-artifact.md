# Business Artifact Map

Capture:

- deliverables requested;
- stakeholders, reviewers, and approvers;
- source-of-truth documents, spreadsheets, systems, emails, or datasets;
- freshness/date of each source;
- calculations, assumptions, and business rules;
- privacy, compliance, or confidentiality constraints;
- acceptance criteria and approval surface;
- prior versions or snapshots for rollback.

Do not treat stakeholder preference as a discoverable fact. Ask only after source materials are inspected.
