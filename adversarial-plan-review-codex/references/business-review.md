# Business Review Lens

Check:

- stakeholders and approvers are identified;
- source-of-truth materials are current and cited;
- assumptions are explicit;
- success criteria match business intent;
- calculations or claims are reproducible;
- privacy and confidentiality constraints are honored;
- approval evidence is defined;
- rollback or prior-version recovery is possible.

Block execution if acceptance depends on an unnamed stakeholder, missing data source, or vague approval.
